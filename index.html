About Us

Founded in 2015, VegExport began with a vision to bridge the gap between local farmers and global markets. What started as a small family-owned business has grown into a leading agricultural export company, serving customers across five continents and partnering with over 200 certified farms.

Our mission is to deliver premium quality vegetables while promoting sustainable farming practices and supporting local communities. We believe in transparency, reliability, and excellence in every aspect of our operations, ensuring that our partners receive nothing but the best in produce and service.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vegetables do you export?

We offer a comprehensive range of both fresh and dry vegetables, including premium tomatoes, onions, garlic, potatoes, and leafy greens. All products are sourced from certified farms and undergo strict quality control.

What are your minimum order quantities?

Our standard MOQ starts at 1000kg per product type, with flexible options for mixed containers. We offer special arrangements for regular buyers and seasonal contracts.

How do you ensure quality during shipping?

We utilize state-of-the-art temperature-controlled containers and advanced packaging technology to maintain optimal freshness. Each shipment is monitored in real-time and includes quality guarantees.

What payment terms do you offer?

We accept various payment methods including L/C, T/T, and D/P. Payment terms are flexible and can be discussed based on order volume and business relationship.

What certifications do you have?

We hold major international certifications including Global GAP, ISO 22000, and HACCP. Our facilities are regularly audited and comply with international food safety standards.
